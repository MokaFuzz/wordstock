Portland's
Book
Festival.

Presented by Bank of America.

Join Literary Arts for Wordstock: Portland’s Book Festival, our annual celebration of literature. This one-day event features local and national authors presenting onstage events, pop-up readings, and workshops. Enjoy activities for readers of all ages, an extensive book fair, local food trucks, entry to the Portland Art Museum and more.

Save the date! The festival returns Saturday, November 10, 2018.

Onstage Events

Onstage events are at the heart of the festival. With over 43 events scheduled for 2017, there’s something for everyone. Learn more about Wordstock’s onstage events.

Authors & Presenters

With over 100 Authors & Presenters on the 2017 lineup, you’re sure to find what you’re looking for, or what you didn’t know you were looking for. Read about Wordstock’s Authors and Presenters.

Workshops

Workshops are a fantastic way to improve one’s craft, meet other amazing local writers, and have a great time. Wordstock 2017 has 15 scheduled workshops. Learn more about Wordstock’s workshops.

Exhibitors & Vendors

The Wordstock book fair represents some of the best of regional and national publishing, writing, literary culture, academic opportunities, and more. Learn more about Wordstock’s exhibitors and vendors.

Partner Events

Wordstock would not be possible without the help from our amazing supporters and community partners. Some of our partners put on additional events during the festival and throughout the year. Learn more about Wordstock's partner events.

Volunteers

Wordstock would not be possible without the help of our incredible volunteers. Volunteering is a great way to experience the festival while connecting with a group of similarly driven readers and writers. Learn more about Wordstock’s volunteer program.
